format is:
u8 opcode  
u32 sequence_number
u32 unk
u8 has_condition
u32 data[repeat until 0xFF FF FF FF]

if has_condition
    u32 condition
    u32 data[repeat until 0xFF FF FF FF]
else
    u32 data[repeat until 0xFF FF FF FF]

opcodes:
    01  Actor say
    08  Emit code
    09  Define state
    0A  Goto state
    64  Choose reply (arg1 = what to say, arg2 = go here if chosen)
